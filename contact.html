Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working together on your next web development project

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take 2-6 months. I provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation and maintain regular communication throughout the development process.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide and am comfortable with different time zones. Communication is key, and I ensure regular updates regardless of location. I have experience working with teams across North America, Europe, and Asia.

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in modern JavaScript frameworks (React, Next.js, Vue.js), Node.js backends, Python applications, cloud platforms (AWS, Google Cloud), and various databases. I choose the best tech stack for each project based on requirements, scalability needs, and budget.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, I offer maintenance and support packages to ensure your application stays secure, updated, and performs optimally. This includes bug fixes, security updates, performance monitoring, and feature enhancements as your business grows.

How do you handle project payments?

I offer flexible payment structures based on project scope. Typically, I work with milestone-based payments: 25% upfront, 50% during development, and 25% upon completion. For smaller projects, we can discuss a 50/50 split. I accept bank transfers, PayPal, and cryptocurrency.
